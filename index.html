KILLING THE RAAVANA

“Don't kill but conquer, the Ravana within you.”

Do you see the negative even in good situations ?

It's PESSIMISM
Replace it with OPTIMISM !

"Pessimism leads to weakness and optimism to power."
An optimistic person thinks the best possible thing will happen, and hopes for it even if it's not likely.

Do you know whom we call a pessimist?
A person who thinks of negativity all the time, the one who only sees negative things around. It’s like looking for doom and sadness in every situation.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Replace Every Negative Thought With a Positive One
• Make a Gratitude List
• Stop Seeing Setbacks as Failures
• Have People Around You That Provide You With Positive Energy
• Do Good Things for Others

Do you find that you offend people a lot?

It's ARROGANCE
Replace it with COURTESY !

"Arrogance makes you stronger from outside but weaker from inside. Stay humble!"
Someone who is courteous is polite and respectful to other people and do not offend others.

Do you know whom we call an arrogant?
A person who behaves in a proud, unpleasant way towards other people because they believe that they are more important than others.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Admit when you’re wrong.
• Learn to laugh at yourself.
• Spend less time worrying about being right.
• Ask other people for help.
• Offer meaningful, realistic compliments.

Do you only do activities that you find convenient and comfortable?

It's LAZINESS
Replace it with AMBITION !

“The only time a lazy man ever succeeds is when he tries to do nothing.”
An ambitious person is someone always striving to reach a goal through hard work and dedication.

Do you know whom we call lazy?
A person who spends most of their time on unproductive things and find it difficult to step out of their comfort zone and make an effort.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Create a plan of action
• Avoid distraction
• Make tedious tasks fun
• Reward yourself
• Don't 'break the chain.'Be consistent.

Do you stick to a point even if you realise it's wrong and you persist on presenting it?

You’re being STUBBORN
Try to be ADAPTIVE !

“Stubbornnes brings either greater humiliation or greater honor”
An adaptive person is flexible — they don't lose their cool when plans change quickly and they are always willing to learn new ways to do things.

Do you know whom we call stubborn?
A person refuses to change their opinion about something or refuses to change their mind about a decision that they've made.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Listen to the other side of the story.
• Keep an open mind by reserving judgement.
• Admit when you’re wrong.
• Remind yourself that you are only human, and that you are not always right.
• Build a supportive environment.
• Be humble.

Do you have a tendency of putting off work till the last minute?

It's PROCRASTINATION
Replace it with PRECRASTINATION !

“Never put off for tomorrow what you can do today.”
A precrastinator finishes tasks ahead of deadlines instead of pushing it to the last moment.

Do you know whom we call a procrastinator?
A person who unnecessarily postpones decisions or actions.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• force yourself to start and to make the first step.
• GET ORGANIZED
• Prioritize your tasks
• SET GOALS and deadlines
• Take breaks when required.Listen to music or practise meditation
• Use positive self-talk

Do you find yourself wondering about unimportant things?

You’re an OVERTHINKER
Replace it with MINDFULNESS !

“Overthinking, also best known as creating problems that are never there.”
Mindfulness is the basic human ability to be fully present, aware of where we are and what we’re doing, and not overly reactive or overwhelmed by anything.

Do you know whom we call an overthinker?
A person who puts too much time into thinking about or analyzing something in a way that is more harmful than helpful.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Change The Story You Tell Yourself
• Let Go of The Past
• LIVE IN THE MOMENT and Practice Being Present
• Focus on What You Can Control
• Manage Your Stress: Move, Unplug, Spend Time in Nature
• Practise mindfulness meditation.

Do you feel unhappy when your peers achieve something that you don't?

You’re being ENVIOUS
Try to be GENEROUS !

“Envy is the art of counting the other fellow's blessings instead of your own.”
A generous person is friendly, helpful, and willing to see the good qualities in someone or something.

Do you know whom we call an envious person?
A person who has feeling of discontent and ill will because of another's advantages, possessions. Envious people can be competitive.They can seem to take pleasure in another's misfortune.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Enjoy your own experiences
• Shift your focus to the goodness in your life.
• Spend less time on social media
• Stop comparing your life with others.
• Avoid people who habitually value the wrong things.

Do you label someone as a “bad person” before you even get to know them ?

You’re being JUDGEMENTAL
Try to be DELIBERATE !

“Never judge a book by it's cover !”
A deliberate person thinks and acts about something carefully without hurrying,considers everything before making any decision.

Do you know whom we call a judgemental person?
A person who often rushes to judgment without reason.Being a judgemental person essentially means thinking, speaking, or behaving in a manner that reflects a critical and condemnatory point of view.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Remember that everyone is doing their best.
• Write down your judgmental thoughts, then reframe them.
• Don't assume when you don't know.
• Be more empathetic.
• Help them rather than judge them.
• Spend more time to know and understand other people better.

Do you feel the urge to take revenge when someone hurts you?

You’re being VINDICTIVE
Try to be FORGIVING !

“Never does the human soul appear so strong as when it foregoes revenge”
Forgiveness doesn't mean forgetting completely.It just means to release feelings of resentment toward a person who has harmed you, regardless of whether they actually deserve your forgiveness. Forgiveness brings a kind of peace that helps you go on with life.

Do you know whom we call a vindictive person?
A person who holds a grudge and who always tries to get back at people who they think have wronged them in some way. They a desire to take revenge and hurt someone who has hurt them or caused problems.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Talk through your feelings.
• Be kind and keep yourself away from the need of revenge.
• Forgive smaller things first
• Realise that carrying anger and seeking revenge consumes your energy and your time.
• Switch the Focus from Blaming Others to Understanding Yourself.
• Learn to Let Go and move on.Forgive & try to forget.

Do you forget things & you're careless of the consequences of your actions?

You’re being IRRESPONSIBLE
Try to be RESPONSIBLE !

“You cannot escape the responsibility of tomorrow by evading it today.”
Being responsible means keeping promises and honoring commitments. It is accepting the consequences for what we say and do.People who are responsible don't make excuses for their actions or blame others when things go wrong.

Do you know whom we call a irresponsible person?
A person who is unlikely to be held accountable for his actions. They do what they like and don't care what happens afterward. Forgetting to perform a given task is irresponsible.
Follow these tips if you have this trait :
• Stop making excuses for yourself.
• Make a schedule to plan your time.
• Be consistent and stick to your schedule.
• Hold yourself accountable for your actions.
• Find solutions for issues instead of casting blame.
• Take care of your tasks before having fun.
